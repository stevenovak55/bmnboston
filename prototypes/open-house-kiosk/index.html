Massachusetts Agency Disclosure

Under Massachusetts law, real estate licensees are required to disclose to consumers the types of agency relationships available:

  • Seller's Agent: Represents the seller and owes fiduciary duties to the seller.
  • Buyer's Agent: Represents the buyer and owes fiduciary duties to the buyer.
  • Dual Agent: Represents both buyer and seller with informed consent.

Today's listing agent represents the seller's interests. If you are not represented by your own buyer's agent, consider seeking representation.
